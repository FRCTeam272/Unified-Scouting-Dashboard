Scoring Calculations

Coral Scoring

The coral score is calculated as a weighted sum of coral placements:

  • Autonomous Period:
    • Level 1: 3 points per coral
    • Level 2: 4 points per coral
    • Level 3: 6 points per coral
    • Level 4: 7 points per coral
  • Teleoperated Period:
    • Level 1: 2 points per coral
    • Level 2: 3 points per coral
    • Level 3: 4 points per coral
    • Level 4: 5 points per coral

Formula: (Auto L13 + Auto L24 + Auto L36 + Auto L47) + (Teleop L12 + Teleop L23 + Teleop L34 + Teleop L45)

Algae Scoring

The algae score has the same weights for both autonomous and teleoperated periods:

  • Autonomous Period:
    • Net Shot: 4 points per algae
    • Processor: 2 points per algae
  • Teleoperated Period:
    • Net Shot: 4 points per algae
    • Processor: 2 points per algae

Formula: (Auto Net Shot4 + Auto Processor2) + (Teleop Net Shot4 + Teleop Processor2)

Endgame Scoring

The endgame score is calculated from the final actions of the match:

  • Park: 2 points
  • Shallow Climb: 6 points
  • Deep Climb: 12 points (shallow climbs are not denoted)

Note: A team can only score one endgame action per match.

Note: All scores shown are averages per match.
