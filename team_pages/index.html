Scoring Calculations

Coral Scoring

The coral score is calculated as a weighted sum of coral placements:

  • Level 1: 1 point per coral
  • Level 2: 2 points per coral
  • Level 3: 3 points per coral
  • Level 4: 4 points per coral

Formula: 2 (Auto L1 + Auto L22 + Auto L33 + Auto L44) + (Teleop L1 + Teleop L22 + Teleop L33 + Teleop L44)

Algae Scoring

The algae score is the sum of algae placements with different weights:

  • Net Shot: 2 points per algae
  • Processor: 1 point per algae

Formula: 2 (Auto Net Shot2 + Auto Processor) + (Teleop Net Shot2 + Teleop Processor)

Note: All scores shown are averages per match. Autonomous period scores are doubled.
